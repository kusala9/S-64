SHOEBURYNESS FIRING DANGER AREAS

Experimental firing is frequently carried out in this area. Apart from certain
exceptions, no vessel may enter or remain in the Inner Danger Area at any time.
Entry to the Outer Danger Area is prohibited when the area is in use as indicated
by red flags. For further information contact Shoeburyness Radar Control on tel:
01702 383260 or see the Range Byelaws. VHF Channel 16 should also be monitored.
