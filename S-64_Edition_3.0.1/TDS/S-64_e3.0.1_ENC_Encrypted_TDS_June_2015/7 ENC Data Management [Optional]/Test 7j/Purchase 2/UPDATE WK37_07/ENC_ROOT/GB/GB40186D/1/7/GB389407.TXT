3894(T)/2007 ENGLAND - West Coast - Saint Ives Bay
- Hayle - Light. Leading line. Works.

Source: Hayle Harbour Master
(SEP: 2007000056607 - 1).

1. Due to a build up of sand in the approaches to
Hayle the front leading light, F.17m4M, in
position 50° 11´·50N., 5° 26´·17W. has been
discontinued and the leading line, bearing 180°,
should not be used until further notice.

2. Dredging is due to commence in late August, for
an unspecified period, to re-instate the channel.

3. This cell will be updated when further details
are available.
