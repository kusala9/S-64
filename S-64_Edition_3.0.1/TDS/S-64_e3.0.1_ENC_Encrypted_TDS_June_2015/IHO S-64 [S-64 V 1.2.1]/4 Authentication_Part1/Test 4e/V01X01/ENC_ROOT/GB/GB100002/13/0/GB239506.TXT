2395(P)/2006 ENGLISH CHANNEL - Western Part - Porthcurno in England to Guernsey and
Guernsey to Lannion in France - Submarine cables.

Source: SEtech Ltd
(HH.226/300/-04 e5).

1. Work has begun on the preparation and laying of cables from Porthcurno in England
to Guernsey and then to Lannion in France. The positions are approximate only, and
are intended to give an indication of the proposed route.

2. A submarine cable is to be laid in this position between
Porthcurno in England and Guernsey.

3. A submarine cable is to be laid in this position between Guernsey and Lannion in
France.
