FRENCH REGULATIONS

Vessels over 1600 GRT laden with hydrocarbons or other dangerous cargoes must
keep at least seven nautical miles off the French coast, except when entering
Roscoff, Brest, Douarnenez, Concarneau, Cherbourg, Ouistreham, Le Havre, Rouen,
Havre-Antifer or Fécamp. For details see Chart 5500 and Admiralty Sailing
Directions.
