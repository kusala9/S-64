ROUTEING

Charted Traffic Separation Schemes are listed in Annual Notice to
Mariners Number 17 (NP247), which is updated annually. It indicates
which schemes are IMO-adopted and includes other relevant
information. For the latest information on individual schemes and
information about IMO mandatory and recommended routes for certain
tankers and other vessels, see Admiralty Sailing Directions.
Near the southern end of the Deep Water route (approximately 52°N)
the least depth found was 26 metres (2000).
In the western Deep Water route via the DR1 light-buoy (53°06'·7N,
02°40'·7E), the least depth was 27 metres (1999), and in the eastern
Deep Water route 23 metres (1995).
