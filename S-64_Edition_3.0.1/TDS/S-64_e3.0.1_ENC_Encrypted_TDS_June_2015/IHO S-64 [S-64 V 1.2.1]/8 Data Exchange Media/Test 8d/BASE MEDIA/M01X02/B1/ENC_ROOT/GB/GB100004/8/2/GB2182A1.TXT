ROUTEING

For information about IMO mandatory and recommended routes for
certain tankers and other vessels, see Admiralty Sailing
Directions.
Near the southern end of the Deep Water route (approximately
52°N) the least depth found was 26m (2000).
In the western Deep Water route, via the DR1 light-buoy
(53°06´·7N, 02°40´·7E), the least depth was 27m (1999), and in the
eastern Deep Water route 23m (1995).
