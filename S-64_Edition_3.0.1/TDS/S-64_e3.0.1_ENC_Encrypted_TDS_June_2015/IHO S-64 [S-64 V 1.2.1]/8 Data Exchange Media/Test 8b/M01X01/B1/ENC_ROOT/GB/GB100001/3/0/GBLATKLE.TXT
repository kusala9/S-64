LADEN TANKERS - TRAFFIC SEPARATION SCHEME OFF LAND'S END

Laden tankers over 10,000 GRT using the Traffic Separation Scheme off Land's 
End, between Seven Stones and Longships, should keep at least 3 nautical miles 
to seaward of Wolf Rock and should not use the Scheme in restricted visibility 
or other adverse weather. For reporting procedures see Admiralty List of Radio 
Signals.
