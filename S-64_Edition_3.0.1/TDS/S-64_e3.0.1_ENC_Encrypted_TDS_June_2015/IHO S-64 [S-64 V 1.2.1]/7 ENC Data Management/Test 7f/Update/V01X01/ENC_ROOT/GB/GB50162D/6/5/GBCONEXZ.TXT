CONTROLLED AND EXCLUSION ZONES

Specified vessels, whilst navigating in the main channel within Milford Haven Port Authority limits,
will be given moving Controlled and Exclusion Zones.

Controlled Zone:
1 mile ahead and 1 mile astern of the specified vessel.

Commercial vessels of length 20 metres or more are not permitted to navigate in this zone without the permission of Port
Control.

Exclusion Zone:
Ahead - the limit of line of sight of the specified vessel, defined by the position of the patrol vessel.
Astern - the stern of the escort tug where provided, or 100 metres from the specified vessel where the tug is not provided.

Vessels are not permitted to navigate in this zone.
