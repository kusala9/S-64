FISHERMAN'S GAT - PRECAUTIONARY AREA

Mariners are warned that within this area, manoeuvring traffic may be encountered
entering or leaving Fisherman's Gat. Extra care should be exercised when
approaching the confluence of Black Deep and Fisherman's Gat and also when
approaching Knock John Channel. Mariners are reminded that advice on shipping
movements in this area is available from Port Control, London on VHF. See
Admiralty List of Radio Signals.
