5319(P)/2005 ENGLAND - West Coast - Saint Ives Bay - Approaches to Hayle -
Beacon. Light-beacon.

Source: Hayle Harbour Master
(HH.220/460/-04 e25)

1. A new port-hand beacon with a red cylindrical topmark has been established
in position 50° 11´·47N., 5° 26´·02W.

2. A green triangular topmark has been added to the existing Q.G light-beacon
in position 50° 11´·43N., 5° 26´·03W.
