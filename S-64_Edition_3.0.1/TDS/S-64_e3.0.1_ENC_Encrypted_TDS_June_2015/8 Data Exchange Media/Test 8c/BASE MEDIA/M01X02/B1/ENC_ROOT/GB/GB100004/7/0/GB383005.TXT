3830(P)/2005  NORTH SEA - United Kingdom Sector - Indefatigable Gas Field - Platform. Buoyage.
Source: Shell UK Exploration and Production & Trinity House Lighthouse Service
(HH - 254 / 437 / -02  E4)


1. A platform in the Indefatigable Gas Field, 49/19M, in position 53° 21' .18N. , 2° 36' .37E. is unlit and 
will remain so until its abandonment is completed in 2007.

2. To mark the platform 4 cardinals buoys have been laid 500m North, East, South and West.

3. Mariners are advised to navigate with caution when in the vicinity.
