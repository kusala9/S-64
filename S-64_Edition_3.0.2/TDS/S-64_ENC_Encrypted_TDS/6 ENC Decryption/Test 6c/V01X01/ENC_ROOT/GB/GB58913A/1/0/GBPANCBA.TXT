BEACHING AREA

This area is safe for beaching vessels in emergencies.

The definition of vessel size is as follows:

Small  - Up to 9144 metres (300 feet) - 549 metres (18 feet) draught.
Medium - Up to 15240 metres (500 feet) - 914 metres (30 feet) draught.
Large  - Over 15240 metres (500 feet) - 9.14 metres (30 feet) draught or over.
