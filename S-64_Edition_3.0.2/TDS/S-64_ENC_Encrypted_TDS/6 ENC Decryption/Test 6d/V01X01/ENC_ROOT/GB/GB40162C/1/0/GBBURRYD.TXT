CHANGEABLE DEPTHS

The depths and channels within Burry Inlet are subject to frequent change. In
August 1992, Middle Channel entrance (51° 38'N, 4° 19'W) was 7 cable north of
the charted position and the line of the channel to Burry Port was defined by
the following points:
  1. 51° 38'·78N, 4° 20'·57W
  2. 51° 38'·78N, 4° 17'·87W
  3. 51° 40'·23N, 4° 15'·07W
