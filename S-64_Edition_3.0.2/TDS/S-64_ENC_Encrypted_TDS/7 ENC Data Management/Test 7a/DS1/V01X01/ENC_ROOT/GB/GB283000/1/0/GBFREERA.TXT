RESTRICTED AREA

The restricted area is of one mile radius centred on the east end of Number 2
Jetty. Vessels entering or leaving Freeport Harbour should keep well clear of
the Borco Oil Terminal and should not pass between the oil jetties and the
shore. No other vessels should enter the restricted area unless authorised to
do so. Anchoring within the restricted area is prohibited. For further
information see Admiralty Sailing Directions.
