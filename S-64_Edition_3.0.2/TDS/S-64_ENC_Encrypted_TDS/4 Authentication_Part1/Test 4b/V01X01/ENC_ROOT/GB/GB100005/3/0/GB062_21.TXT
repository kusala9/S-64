WRECKS

1.  All wrecks are omitted within the 30 metre line and in depths greater than
200 metres in this area.

2(a).  Elsewhere, all wrecks with a least or safe clearance depth considered
to be greater than 18 metres are shown as non-dangerous.

2(b).  However, where the least or clearance depth over a wreck is between 18
and 30 metres and is known, the depth will be given.
