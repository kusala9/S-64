2545(T)/2006 WALES - South Coast - Milford Haven - South Hook Marine
Terminal - Buoy.

Source: Milford Haven Port Authority Notice 49/06
(HH.214/410/-22 e5).

1. Mariners are advised that a special light-buoy, Fl.Y.10s, has been deployed
in approximate position 51° 41´·89N., 5° 05´·16W. marking a group of piles.

2. Mariners are requested to exercise caution and not approach within 100m of
the South Hook LNG Terminal and associated structures.
