HUMBER DEEP WATER ANCHORAGE

Mariners are advised that this anchorage is recommended for large vessels bound 
for the Humber. Mariners are further advised that good holding ground is in the 
charted designated anchorage Numbers 1, 2 and 3. Mariners are advised to keep 
their vessels in a state of readiness and be prepared to get underway at short 
notice.
